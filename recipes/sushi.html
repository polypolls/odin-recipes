California Roll

Description

The recipe contains every step to make restaurant-ready California Rolls. There is everything to know, from ingredients to a step by step instruction.

Ingredients

  • Red Caviar
  • Rice
  • Avocado
  • Salmon

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Flatten out rice on cooking table
  3. Put Avocado and Salmon on top of the rice
  4. Make a roll
  5. Roll the roll in caviar, for it to stick
