California Roll

Description

The recipe contains every step to make restaurant-ready Croissant. There is everything to know, from ingredients to a step by step instruction.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix Butter, Milk and Eggs
  2. Wait for dough to get bigger
  3. Sequentially add Butter to the dough
  4. After every new sequence put the dough for a couple of hours into the fridge
  5. Bake the dough
