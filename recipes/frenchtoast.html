French Toast

Description

The recipe contains every step to make restaurant-ready French Toast. There is everything to know, from ingredients to a step by step instruction.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix Butter, Milk and Eggs
  2. Put the liquid dough into the frying pan
  3. Put some oil on the oil as it gets some bigger in the frying pan
  4. Add some blueberries and strawberries to the plate
  5. Finish of by putting the ready french toast onto the plate
